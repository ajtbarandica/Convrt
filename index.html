FAQ

Questions answered.

How long does it take to build my website? +
Depending on the package: the Starter website is delivered in 7–10 business days, the Pro + AI Chat in 14–18 days, and the Premium package in 21–28 days. We move fast because we use AI-powered development tools — without sacrificing quality.
What do I need to provide to get started? +
We'll send you a simple onboarding form and an asset checklist. The essentials are: your logo, photos of your business or team, your list of services, and access to your domain (if you have one). If you're missing any of these, we'll tell you exactly how to handle it — we've been through this before.
What is the AI chatbot, exactly? +
It's a smart chat widget installed on your website, trained specifically on your business — your services, pricing, hours, FAQs, and service area. When a visitor asks a question, the bot answers instantly. It also captures their name, phone, and email, and can book appointments directly to your calendar. You receive an instant notification for every new lead.
Do I own the website once it's delivered? +
Yes, 100%. You own all the content, design, and code. The monthly plan covers hosting, security, updates, and support — but you're never locked in. If you cancel, you keep everything and can move your site to any host you choose.
What if I already have a website? +
We can add an AI chatbot to any existing website regardless of platform — WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom code. If you want a new site, we'll build it from scratch. If you're not sure, book a free call and we'll give you an honest assessment.
What does the monthly plan include? +
The monthly plan covers managed hosting, daily backups, security monitoring, platform updates, and technical support with a 48-hour response time. Higher-tier plans also include chatbot operation and updates, minor content edits, and monthly performance reports. Think of us as your ongoing digital team.
